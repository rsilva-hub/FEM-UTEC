Course Overview

Curso teórico-práctico orientado a los fundamentos del método de elementos finitos y la simulación no lineal de estructuras especiales, integrando conceptos teóricos con aplicaciones computacionales para ingeniería estructural.

Lecturers

Instructors and teaching fellows with experience in finite element modeling, nonlinear analysis and advanced structural applications.

Venue

Information about the host institution, location, facilities and infrastructure where the course will take place.

Organization

Organised by a multidisciplinary group of researchers associated with academic and research institutions working in structural engineering, computational mechanics and applied numerical methods.
