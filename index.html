Course Overview

Curso teórico-práctico orientado a los fundamentos del método de elementos finitos y la simulación no lineal de estructuras especiales, con énfasis en aplicaciones avanzadas en ingeniería estructural y mecánica computacional.

LECTURERS

Instructors and teaching fellows with experience in finite element modeling, nonlinear analysis, and advanced structural applications.

VENUE

Universidad de Ingeniería y Tecnología (UTEC). Modern facilities and advanced infrastructure for teaching and research.

ORGANIZATION

Organised by a multidisciplinary group of researchers associated with academic and research institutions working in structural engineering and computational mechanics.
